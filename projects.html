OMGPOP

April 2011-April 2012

At OMGPOP, I worked on marketing ad campaigns for mobile, email and itunes store, game UI design in both mobile and web, and website redesign. I was lucky to work on DrawSomething which quickly became a hit game, ranked top free and top grossing game in March 2012 and amassed over 100 million downloads in it’s first year of existence. The popular appeal of DrawSomething resulted in OMGPOP to sell to Zynga for $160 million in April 2012.

Matchpuppy

December 2010-April 2012

I co-founded Matchpuppy. We started it as a fun side project for our dogs. We created a web app for dog owners to make profiles for their dog and based on similar characteristics, dog owners were able to find play dates for their dogs and meet other cool dog owners in their area. I designed the website, web app and all marketing materials. We gave out stickers and cards in dog parks, talked to dog owners and spread the word about our product. We grew our user base to 10,000 dog owners.

Vantageous

April 2012 - December 2012

I worked with Tim, the founder and CEO of Vantageous (currently called Fly. www.editonthefly.com/fly) on building his video editing mobile app. I met him at DreamIt Ventures in New York where I worked as the in house designer for a few months to help their portfolio companies. I designed UI comps for the iphone, Android and iPad app. Vantageous is a multi angle video recording app that allows you to easily cut and paste different video segments to create professional quality videos.
